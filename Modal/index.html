Call for Application as Mentee


Eligibility:

Mentee is open free of charge to students of agricultural science in any Universities, Polytechnics and Colleges of Education and youths who are involved in agriculture as a profession in Nigeria. Presently, our offices are located in Federal University of Technology, Akure, Ondo State and University of Nigeria, Nsukka, Enugu State, Nigeria.


Benefit as mentee:

As a mentee, you will enjoy free counselling on improvement of your academic pursuit through proper planning and monitoring, free information on career requirements and research opportunities through networking and motivational talks to improve your competence and confidence levels. Finally, as a mentee, you will have access to workshop and training development courses organized for mentees by WAMP.


Application process:

Screening of candidates will be at different stages.
Both successful and unsuccessful candidates will be notified at the end of the screening.


Deadline:
Application will be open to prospective mentee throughout the year but selection will be done twice in a year.
